Welcome to the Road Rules Permit Practice

Learn road rules while navigating the highway!

Rules of the Game:

  • Follow traffic signals (Stop at red, slow down at yellow, and go on green).
  • Obey speed limits. Slow down to 20 mph in school zones and at speed bumps.
  • Stop completely at stop signs before proceeding.
  • Avoid collisions with cones, roadwork zones, or obstacles.
  • Navigate safely and reach the finish line to win the game.
